# 💫 About Me:
I am a Software Engineering student at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC) with a strong passion for backend development and building efficient, scalable systems. While my foundation is in server-side problem-solving, I am currently evolving into a Fullstack Developer to master the entire development lifecycle. This transition allows me to understand how every component integrates, enabling me to design more robust and high-quality software. I am a natural builder who loves collaborating on community projects and experimenting with diverse technologies to see how they fit into the bigger picture. Driven by continuous learning, I aim to build software that perfectly balances strong logic with a seamless user experience.
